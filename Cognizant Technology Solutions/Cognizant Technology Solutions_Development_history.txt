Cognizant Technology Solutions's year founded: 1994.0

Cognizant Technology Solutions's reason for establishment:
    - The origins of Cognizant Technology Solutions Corporation trace back to 1994, when it was established as a division of Dun & Bradstreet Corporation. It was the vision of its founders, Kumar Mahadeva and Francisco D'Souza, to create a company that would deliver high-quality software development services.
