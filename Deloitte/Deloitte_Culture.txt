Deloitte's key values:
    - Lead the way: Deloitte is not only leading the profession but also reinventing it for the future. The company is committed to creating opportunities and leading the way to a more sustainable world.
    - Serve with integrity: Deloitte acts ethically and with integrity, earning the trust of clients, regulators, and the public. Upholding that trust is the company's single most important responsibility.
    - Take care of each other: Deloitte looks out for one another and prioritizes respect, fairness, development, and well-being.
    - Foster inclusion: Deloitte is at its best when fostering an inclusive culture and embracing diversity in all forms. This approach attracts top talent, enables innovation, and helps deliver well-rounded client solutions.
    - Collaborate for measurable impact: Deloitte approaches work with a collaborative mindset, teaming across businesses, geographies, and skills to deliver tangible, measurable, and attributable impact.

Deloitte's community significance:
    - Our commitment to ethics and integrity: Deloitte aims for the highest standards in ethics and integrity. The company's conduct and accountability have earned the trust of clients, communities, and its own employees. Maintaining that trust is Deloitte’s single most important responsibility.

    - Our commitment to human rights: Deloitte believes that all people are born free and equal in dignity and rights. The company’s Human Rights statement affirms its commitments and approach to respecting human rights within the organization.

    - Our commitment to society and the environment: Deloitte believes it has a responsibility to be a force for good and to lead on the increasingly complex challenges society faces. WorldImpact is Deloitte’s portfolio of initiatives focused on making a tangible impact on society’s biggest challenges and creating a more sustainable and equitable world.

    - Making an impact with technology: Deloitte recognizes that technology provides tremendous opportunities to fulfill its Purpose, enabling it to serve clients with distinction, provide employees with a leading talent experience, and contribute to the communities in which it operates. The company has developed a set of ethical technology principles to guide its choices, considering both the ethical implications of its tech-related activities and the potential impact on end users and society.
